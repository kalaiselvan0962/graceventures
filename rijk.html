Essential Safety Tips

1

Stay Aware

Avoid distractions like earphones while walking alone, especially at night.

2

Share Your Route

Always inform a friend/family about your travel route and expected arrival time.

3

Trust Your Instincts

If a situation feels unsafe, leave immediately and seek help without hesitation.

4

Self-Defense Basics

Carry a safety whistle or pepper spray (where legal) & learn basic escape moves.
